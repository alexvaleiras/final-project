ALEX VALEIRAS

Rain

Small hard to distinguish but can feel them as each droplet falls and wondering why it wont stop. The mind overflows with unintelligent and well thought out remarks just in case you have to feel vulnerable or be in control to the slave that whips and screams each scene must be perfect then as a complete war breaks loose between right, wrong, conscious, or brain lifting all weight from my shoulders and I can feel the ultimate weightless feel of life and how liberating one breath really feels.
